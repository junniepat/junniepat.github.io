Yolla

We designed an app with HD-quality calling and low rates.

The challenge

We participated in the brand development and design of Yolla, an app for a direct communication provider. Our job was to enter the social networking market with an ultra-modern approach and present users with the best connection for in-app calls. Already the app boasts millions of users as our fresh design fulfills its functions.

The project

Yolla equips its users with the novel opportunity to make international calls and chat for less money. It’s a popular service thanks to its guiding philosophy: a high-quality connection, low rates, no hidden fees, no installation fees, and no advertising.
