YourSportAgent

Development of a web service for searching professional athletes.

the challenge

We were faced with the task of developing a sophisticated, multi-functional web platform that boasts a sea of possibilities for searching perspective players, watching hockey match videos, and features footage of individual players with a detailed breakdown of their playing style. Such a large project necessitated breaking up development into several stages and every step of the journey was accomplished by Cuberto’s talented team.

the project

Yoursportagent is a trailblazing scouting system which supports multiple roles: clubs, players, scouts, agents, and trainers. Each identity involves its own distinctive functionality. The system’s development was guided by the double goals of centralizing and formulating a player database and hockey matches for the purposes of increasing international hockey qualifications.
