James Kadi

Vision

James Kadi, MD, Plastic and Reconstructive Surgeon, board-certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgeons is committed to provide the best cosmetic and reconstructive surgery experience for his patients. Dr. Kadi and the patient will review goals together to identify and achieve patient's requested goals safely and

with utmost privacy from initial consultations through procedures and post- operative care and follow-ups. Dr. Kadi uses the most current, proven and appropriate treatments available.

Dr. Kadi strives to earn your trust and at the heart of the practice is the personal care and support he and his staff provides. Dr. Kadi has extensive experience with breast augmentation, breast augmentation and lift, breast reduction, abdominoplasty (tummy tuck), brachioplasty, face lift and thigh lift.

Dr. Kadi and staff are dedicated to providing his patients with outstanding, compassionate care while continuing a lifetime commitment to surgical excellence.


About

Dr. Kadi was born in Kansas City, Kansas, moved to Baton Rouge, Louisiana when he was seven years old and went on to be awarded a football scholarship to LSU. After college he attended LSU school of dentistry where he graduated and went on to earn a Masters degree in periodontics from the University of North Carolina, Chapel Hill, North Carolina.

Dr. Kadi rt.< worked as a periodontist in Baton Rouge for six years. He subsequently went back to LSU medical school in New Orleans when he was 33 years old. Upon graduating from medical school he did two years of general surgery training at Methodist Hospital in Dallas as part of his requirement for completing a five year residency in Head and Neck surgery/Ear, Nose and Throat surgery from the Medical Center of LSU in New Orleans.

After completing the Head and Neck/Ear, Nose and Throat surgery training he then completed two years of Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery training from Case Western Reserve in Cleveland, Ohio at the University Hospitals of Cleveland and then came directly to Texas in 1997.

Dr. Kadi considers himself a true Texan. He is also a very proud parent of three boys. Dr. Kadi has been servicing the areas of Denton, Lewisville and Flower Mound since that time.
