Visit Idaho!

The gem of the North-west, Whether you’re a newcomer or a Gem State guru… There’s an adventure for you in Idaho.

Did you know that over 60% of Idaho is designated public land? With over 3,100 recreational river miles, Idaho is the king of whitewater. Whether it’s a 7-day trip on the world-famous Middle Fork of the Salmon River or a day trip on the Cabarton stretch of the Payette River, Idaho offers an abundance of rafting opportunities to get your adrenaline pumping, and of course, sit back and revel in the amazing scenery on those flat stretches. Outfitters are located all across the state and can set you up on a trip suited for your thrill level.
