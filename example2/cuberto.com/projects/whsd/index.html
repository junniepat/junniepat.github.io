WHSD SPb

Design and development of the official app for the WHSD tollway.

The challenge

WHSD SPb, a highway which allows access to St. Petersburg’s city center, was successfully launched in 2017. This was a huge transaction in road construction involving both public and private financing and our company was selected for developing and maintaining WHSD’s mobile application.

The project

We can definitively say that participating in this project was a one-of-a-kind opportunity, especially given the scale of construction and operation of a roadway in a huge metropolis with a population of 5 million. The difficulty lay in lengthy negotiations concerning this or that decision and we strive to maintain and improve the product while keeping in mind user preferences.
