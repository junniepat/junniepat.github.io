Colorfly

Redesigned a popular coloring book with copyrighted drawings.

the challenge

It’s not every day that we get requests for designing apps for coloring books, especially from China. The first version of the app was launched in 2015 with an overwhelming bias to the actual coloring pages. After establishing a successful marketing campaign and a target audience, the client approached Cuberto for an image refresh since the previous design had grown stale.

the project

The market is already chock full of similar applications and Colorfly leads the vivid, international array of coloring apps. The vibrant application has an enormous collection of one-of-a-kind illustrations, and it’s easy to create your very own masterpiece in just 10 minutes.
