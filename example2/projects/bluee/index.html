Bluee

Rebranding and design for an international calling app.

The challenge

One of our first projects in the social networking sphere involved a total brand and design overhaul. Bluee continues to provide technological support to its users, but the design remains the same and even after all these years, it’s slick and on-trend.

The project

Bluee is a pro at delivering cheap international calls and texts. Everything in the app guarantees a quick and easy way to make a call, check your balance, find rates, and choose the best plan for you. Best of all, Bluee boasts an extraordinary connection with extensive coverage – the company has been at it since 2012 and definitely knows what it’s doing.
