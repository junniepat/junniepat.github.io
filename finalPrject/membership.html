Join the Chamber

Benefits of Joining

Corporate Exposure/Visibility: Through Publications, Marketing and Referrals, members businesses are brought to limelight.

Access to Government Programmes and Opportunities: Information from Government easily get to members via the chamber of commerce.

Business Support: Various advocacy programmes as well as proposals to Government and International institutions enable members businesses to receive external support such as loans, grants etc.

Technology Transfer and Business Idea Stimulation: Being a member of a chamber of commerce provides useful platform to access some industrial technologies, ideas and economic opportunities.

Gain a Voice in Government: The Chamber of Commerce as the umbrella that houses industrialists helps protect the principles of free enterprises and aid businesses in being treated fairly. It also takes on the tough issues and opposes new regulations, taxes, and fees of the government that are directed towards businesses.

Mentoring: The chamber is a veritable place for small business start-ups to find a more knowledgeable and experienced person in their proposed line of business to guide.

Trade Documentation: The chamber of commerce facilitates some basic trade documentation for members such as issuance of Certificate of Origin and other Int’l documentations at subsidized rates.

Other Business Services: Recovering of debts from overseas and local business associates, settlement of international and local business disputes among business associates/partners, attracting government appointments/award to members in recognition of their outstanding business achievements, registration and incorporation of business names etc.
