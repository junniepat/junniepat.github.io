Ritmer

Design and development of a medical startup for ECG monitoring.

The challenge

There’s nothing more inspiring than projects involving wellness, health, and medical technologies, and we participate in them with great pleasure. For Ritmer, our team developed a design and landing page for a mobile personal cardiograph app. This high-tech device works along with the application and analyzes your EСG.

The project

Ritmer is your very own cardiograph and includes an automatic analysis of your EСG for remotely monitoring your cardiovascular system and the functional state of your body. This unique product was launched in 2017 and is intended for anyone who cares about their health. The device attaches to the chest with electrodes and registers the heart’s signals.
