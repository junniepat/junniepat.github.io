Target Audience

The target audience for Metrologg.com are mostly people who do outdoor activities e.g construction companies, vendors at the local market shed, journalists, students, professors, academics, scientists, agricultural business, event business, builders, engineers, and much more.

Age: 12 to 100 years

Technology: We used modern technologies and have slightly modified view for different ages accessing the website to make it intuitive and easy to use.
Income: Income levels will typically be between $0 - $100,000 annually, as we have free and paid plans.
Motivation: Improve knowledge of mainstream climate technologies and the best applications of Metrologg in the enterprise, organization, or educational establishment.
