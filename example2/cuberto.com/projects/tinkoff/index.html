Tinkoff Bank

Maintenance and design of bank products for promo purposes.

The challenge

One of the best and biggest online banks in Russia, Tinkoff Bank emphasizes not only excellent customer service, but also marketing. With a collaboration history that goes way back, Cuberto has helped Tinkoff generate multiple digital products aimed at advertising and promoting the bank’s latest offerings.

The project

Tinkoff Bank, Russia’s commercial bank, focuses exclusively on remote services and lacks retail outlets. The bank makes generous use of automation systems for realizing its business processes. The bank’s core products include the Tinkoff Platinum credit card, the Tinkoff Black cashback card, and the ALL Airlines credit card.
