DSX

Creation of a trading cryptocurrency exchange platform.

the challenge

In early 2017, we received a request from DSX, a London - based financial company, to develop a trading platform for cryptocurrency exchange from ground zero. This was DSX’s entry into the global marketplace in response to the skyrocketing cryptocurrency demand. After negotiating and coordinating the technical task ahead of us, we rolled up our sleeves and got to work.

the project

DSX is based on attracting and deploying professional experience on the cryptocurrency market, heralding a ground-breaking new era of cryptocurrency exchange. The system has an intricate, multiuser functionality, like any trading platform. Thanks to our extensive experience, we knew exactly what we had to do.
