James Hall to Speak at Reasons to be Creative Event

Reasons to be Creative is a 3 day conference held from 7-9th September 2015 with a festival vibe, held annually in Brighton UK.

Every year the very best international creative and developer speakers take to the stage to wow, inspire, inform, entertain, thrill, teach and mix with web designers and coders that attend from all over the world.

Among the full speakers list you’ll see my face!

John Davey from Reasons.to had this to say about the talk:

When James Hall spoke at Reasons:London – he was an immediate sensation. Grown adults clutched their mouths in horror as James talked about how easy it is to spy on peoples handheld device screens, the SVG hack, and how easy it is to grab passwords! When it comes to understanding about security over the Internet, how to be more vigilant and secure, James is the man!

He has worked in the digital sector for 12 years, and is the author of the popular jsPDF library , and is a founder/Director of Parallax, a digital agency in the UK. Parallax have produced fantastic work for the likes of British Airways , Nespresso , Hisense and many more.

If you are at all concerned about privacy, security and your own sanity, do not miss James at Reasons to be Creative.

Now all I have to do is live up to the hype! :)

Viewing the full event schedule, it’s clear that if you are passionate about the craft that goes into your work, then Reasons to be Creative is for you; with 3 tracks over 3 full days there really is something for everyone.

The people behind the event have curated it knowing that today’s professionals are multi-disciplined, and draw their inspiration from many different influences. That’s why this event brings together the world’s most talented designers, coders, creatives, film makers, animators, installation builders, illustrators, and artists.

I am really looking forward to being a part of us such an exciting event and I hope to see you there!
